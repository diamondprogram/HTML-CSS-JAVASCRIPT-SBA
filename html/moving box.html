In this demo we are using percentages for the animation. Each percentages repersent the CSS at a different point of the animation. You may have notice tha is moves and changes colors, but slowly comes to a stop at 4 points. each of those points represent the box completing one of the percentage keyframes before is moves on to the next frame.
0% is the first and starting frame of the animation. It starts at it place of Origin and is the color red.
25% the box slides to the left 200px and it color transitions from red to yellow.
50% the box slides down 200px and it colors changes to blue.
At 75% the box slides back 200px and changes it color to green.
Finally at 100% the box slides back up 200px and now it's back in it place of orign and it color is back to being red.
